Refinement Principles

  • Literal recognition: Icons must pass the "0.5 second glance test"
  • Book: Clear spine on left, visible page edges, bookmark for detail
  • Film Camera: Vintage 16mm/35mm camera with body, lens, film reels, viewfinder
  • Film Reel: Six radiating spokes, center hub, film strip perforations
  • Theater Masks: Classic comedy/tragedy double mask with oval shapes and distinct expressions
  • Television: Retro TV with rabbit ear antennas, control knobs, visible screen
  • Video Game: Ergonomic controller with D-pad, action buttons, shoulder triggers
  • Musical Note: Staff lines with treble clef and eighth notes

What Changed

  • Book: Transformed from single page to closed book with spine and multiple page edges
  • Film Camera: Added film reels on top, prominent circular lens, viewfinder detail
  • Film Reel: Increased spokes to 6, added film strip perforations, clearer hub
  • Theater Masks: Changed from single square mask to iconic overlapping comedy/tragedy masks
  • Television: Enhanced with rabbit ear antennas and control knobs for instant recognition
  • Video Game: Improved controller shape with clear D-pad and colored action buttons
  • Musical Note: Added staff lines and treble clef for musical context

Maintained Aesthetic

  • Geometric shapes and symmetry preserved throughout
  • 2-3 color palette per icon maintained
  • 2-3px stroke weights for clarity at all sizes
  • Wes Anderson vintage feel with improved functional clarity
  • Icons remain cohesive with unchanged icons (Crown, Trophy, Clock, etc.)
